Our Products

MedSense SecretScript

Designed for forensic marking and anti-counterfeiting, SecretScript is used to covertly tag documents, products, or materials. The ink remains invisible under normal lighting and becomes visible only under specific conditions, making it ideal for verifying authenticity and tracing items during investigations.

Typical Use Cases:
  • Pharmaceutical packaging: Hidden security codes to authenticate medication.
  • Luxury goods: Covert markers or invisible barcodes to guarantee product authenticity.
  • Legal documents & forensic samples: Tagging evidence and documents for secure inspection only under specific conditions.

MedSense DetectTB

An innovative point-of-care diagnostic tool designed to identify active tuberculosis (TB) early — within just 30 minutes — using a simple blood serum sample.
