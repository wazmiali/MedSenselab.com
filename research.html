Bioprinting Innovations

Cryobioprinting

Cryobioprinting integrates printing and cryopreservation to create cell-laden structures that can be stored and used later. This innovative process simplifies biofabrication and enhances tissue shelf-life, showing great potential for medical applications.

Learn more in Matter

Multi-material Bioprinting

Multi-material bioprinting enables the creation of complex, functional tissues by combining different bioinks. This approach supports diverse applications, from tissue engineering to personalized medicine.

Explore in Advanced Materials

Composite Hydrogels

Composite hydrogels are advanced materials that enhance cell growth and tissue repair. Their versatility makes them ideal for robotics, drug delivery, and regenerative medicine.
