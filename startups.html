MedSense India Pvt. Ltd.

MedSense India Pvt. Ltd. is a startup incubated at MedSense Lab, IIT Jammu. The startup focuses on cutting-edge biomedical sensing technology, offering innovative solutions such as rapid diagnostics, smart biosensors, and electroceuticals to improve healthcare delivery in both clinical and remote settings.
