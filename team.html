Dr. Vinay Sharma

Team Leader

Our research group focuses on nanotechnology applications in bioengineering, with an emphasis on developing innovative technologies at the nanoscale and exploring their biomedical applications. Key areas of research include:

1.Technology Development at Nanoscale: Portable Diagnostics, Biosensors, Single molecule/Single Cell Techniques etc.

2. Biomedical Applications of Nanostructures: Bioimaging, AMR, Cancer Therapy, Gene Therapy etc.

With a strong interdisciplinary approach, our lab integrates biotechnology, nanotechnology, electronics, chemistry, and materials science to create healthcare solutions that are both accessible and affordable.
